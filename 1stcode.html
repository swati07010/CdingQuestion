New Registration

Procedure for New Registration

  1. Read the instructions given below carefully before clicking on "Register" button.
  2. Enter the basic information and select required fields and click on "Continue" button.
  3. Enter the Qualification, Skill set, Experience, National & International Language. While entering remember the following:
    1. Add your all Qualification(s)n chronological order with Highest Degree First. Candidate will be sponsored on the basis of the added Qualifications only.
    2. The data added to list will be submitted. Only selection of data does not mean that data will be submitted.
    3. After selecting the data you MUST CLICK ON "ADD TO LIST" button.
    4. You can enter maximum of 5 records in each category.
    5. Skill set is an auto-complete field, wait to populate. As you will type data field will be populated and you have to select from the appearing list by clicking on desired data.

Important Instructions/Points to remember

  1. The fields marked * are mandatory.
  2. Please check all the details and spelling before submitting the page. The Department will not be responsible for the errors at the User-end.
  3. The verification of details provided by the User is not the responsibilty of the Department.
  4. The Registration Process consists of TWO Steps: Providing Basic Details and Providing Qualification & Experience Details.
  5. In the first Form, Please fill in the mandatory fields (Point 1).
  6. In the second Form, enter your qualification, experience and language details.
  7. Click on 'Add' to enter further details.
